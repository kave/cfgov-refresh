Write your own letter

If you are writing your own letter, make certain to include, at a minimum, the information below. Our sample letter can provide more guidance.

A request should include:

  • Your contact information (e.g., full name, address, phone number, email address).
  • As many details as possible about the information you are seeking from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (e.g., “Consumer complaints received between January 1, 2013 and the present that were related to overdraft charges”).
  • Type of requester. For example: “for commercial purposes,” “news media,” “non-commercial scientific institution,” “educational institution,” or “other.”
  • Indicate whether you are willing to pay fees for searching, reviewing, and/or duplication records. You may request that fees be waived because the request is not commercial and it will significantly contribute to public understanding of the operations or activities of the government; please make sure you include a waiver justification for us to consider.
